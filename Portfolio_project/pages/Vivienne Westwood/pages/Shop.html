Rust Lelio Jacket

£3,175.00 £1,270.00

One of the strongest looks of this Spring/Summer 2016 collection catwalk show, the embellished Rust Lelio Jacket is inspired by the concept of recycling objects and different materials, reusing them as “embroideries”. Combining big shoulders and a fluid, slim fit, the jacket features real buttons, hardware and discarded toys stitched on the front. Crafted in an exquisite cupro and silk twill that have been cold dyed for a weathered and unique appeal, pair yours with the Rust Lily Trousers and the Black Towering Ankle Sandals for a true Westwood statement look.Please note that a cold dye process have been applied to the garments for a “one of a kind” look that makes every single garment different from the other as a desired effect. Please also note that the embroideries and applications will vary from jacket to jacket and no two garments will be the same. Should you require any assistance with this please do not hesitate to contact our Customer Service Team.
